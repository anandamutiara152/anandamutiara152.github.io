About Us

skin care driven by curiosity and science What if. Two little words with endless possibilities

  • 'What if' brings a much-needed change to skincare. It surprises us. It intrigues us. And it opens our minds to something never done before.
  • What if we melted a bottle of cream into a toner? What if we crushed a lip balm with a sleeping mask? What if we broke down hyaluronic acid so it could penetrate deeper into the skin? dalam?
  • 'What if' has the power to make the complex simple. It frees us from rigid rules. It gives us the possibility to easily feel like ourselves.

At Laneige, our curiosity leads us to explore new frontiers in beauty because we believe there’s always a better, simpler, and more enjoyable way to care for our skin. In a world full od old skincare rules, we invite you to join us in asking “What If?” - What if we tried things differently, broke the norms, and let ourselves be open to wonder? #OPENTOWONDER
